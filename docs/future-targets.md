## Data Availability Matrix

### Breakthrough Listen Archive
- GBT data: 1-12 GHz coverage, most nearby stars
- Parkes data: 0.7-4 GHz, southern sky targets
- APF data: Optical spectra, bright stars

### FAST SETI Data
- Limited public availability
- Contact Tao et al. for collaboration

### VLA/ALMA
- Not typically used for SETI
- Useful for follow-up of candidates

## Prioritization Criteria

1. **Distance:** Closer is better (higher sensitivity)
2. **Planet confirmation:** Known HZ planets prioritized
3. **Data availability:** Archival data enables immediate analysis
4. **Unique science:** First search of target valuable regardless of result

## Observation Requests

If gaps identified, consider requesting new observations:
- Breakthrough Listen accepts target proposals
- SETI Institute ATA available for coordination
- FAST international time allocation
