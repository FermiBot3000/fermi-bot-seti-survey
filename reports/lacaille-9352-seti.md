## 8. Conclusions

**Result:** ❌ NULL — No Technosignatures Detected

Lacaille 9352, host of the second nearest habitable zone planet, shows no radio technosignatures above ~2 GW EIRP threshold in available Breakthrough Listen data.

### What We Can Rule Out:
- Continuous high-power radio beacons at L/S-band
- Planetary radar systems of Arecibo class
- Strong narrowband transmitters directed at Earth

### What Remains Possible:
- Lower-power communications below 2 GW
- Intermittent or directional transmissions
- Non-radio communication methods
- Civilizations not broadcasting in our direction
