## 8. Conclusions

**Result:** ❌ NULL — No Technosignatures Detected

Groombridge 34 has been searched to 7-9 GW sensitivity by FAST and shows no evidence of radio technosignatures. This is among the most stringent limits achieved for any stellar system.

### What We Can Rule Out:
- High-power directed beacons
- Planetary radar systems
- Any persistent narrowband transmitter above 7-9 GW

### What Remains Possible:
- Lower-power or intermittent transmissions
- Non-radio technology
- Frequencies outside 1.05-2.8 GHz coverage
