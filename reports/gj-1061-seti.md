## 8. Conclusions

**Result:** ❌ NULL — No Technosignatures Detected

Despite hosting two habitable zone planets around a quiet star with extensive observational coverage, GJ 1061 shows no radio technosignatures in the Breakthrough Listen data.

### What We Can Rule Out:
- Continuous high-power radio beacons (>280 GW) directed at Earth
- Active planetary radar systems
- Persistent narrowband transmitters in L/S-band
- Intermittent transmissions occurring during any of 90+ observation windows

### What Remains Possible:
- Lower-power transmissions (<280 GW EIRP)
- Broadband or spread-spectrum signals
- Non-radio communications (optical, neutrino)
- Intermittent signals not coinciding with observations
- Civilizations not transmitting in our direction
